Week one at Akirachix

The warm welcome given to us by the alumni was the best part ,it felt like I was not new, rather a family member.
Given another chance I will still reapply to Akirachix .
An detailed orientation was done to our parents and us to help us understand school's policies and procedures, meet with advisors, and get acclimated to the campus.



After laying down a few casual activities I'd love nothing more than to express my learning journey which has been beyond mind blowing.
  1. The class sessions has been interactive.
  2. Both trainers and technical assistance are supportive and willing to help.
  3. The pratical assigments given to us are good helping us understanding the concepts being taught.
Although I have had amazing experiences most of the time some of my negative experiences are:
  • Cold showers through the nights and some mornings.
  • Lack of co-curriculum activities to help us take a break from the studies .
Having said that, the reputation of this organization precedes the characters hence the magnificent rankings.
